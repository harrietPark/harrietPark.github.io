Lucid Dream

C# UnityEngine VR Vive Game



Walkthrough Video


Lucid Dream was an individual project from South Korea's government-sponsored VR boot camp. This app is a VR game made with Unity Engine and developed for HTC Vive. The main goal of the game is to escape from the nightmare. The player has to find a "Dream Catcher" from the journey of a dream composed of five stages. One has to complete a mission from each stage to get to the next one.
