Project Overview

In this prototype, I explored how AI-driven narratives can create personalised, emotional experiences in XR. The experience centers on a boy‚Äôs memories of a lost relationship. By simply looking at certain objects in the scene, users can trigger an emotional moment that unfolds in real time.
