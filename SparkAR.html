2. VR Magic

Walkthrough Video


VR Magic IG Filter was group coursework for the Augmented and Mixed Realities module. Team members were Anna-Louise Blewden, Holly Clibbon, Sean Hangstrom, Junyi Zou, and myself. Our group wanted to express that by using AR technology, people can go everywhere, and do whatever they want to do like magic.

Technology used:
- Animating 3D Objects
- Head Tracking
- Touch detections
- Mouth Tracking
- Background Segmentation
